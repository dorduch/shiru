Parent Edit Screen — Portrait Layout

The edit screen has a form + card preview. How should they stack in portrait?

A — Preview on Top, Form Below

Card preview at the top, scrollable form below. User sees their card live as they fill the form by scrolling up. Clean separation, natural mobile flow.

B — Tabbed (Edit / Preview)

Two tabs: "Edit" shows the form, "Preview" shows the card. Maximizes space for each. But requires an extra tap to see the card — interrupts the feedback loop.

C — Keep Side-by-Side

Same layout on all sizes, just compressed. Preview stays small on the left, form on the right. Consistent across orientations but cramped on small phones — preview becomes tiny.
